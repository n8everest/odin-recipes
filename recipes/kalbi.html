Kalbi (Korean BBQ Short Ribs

A Savory flavored Korean Rib Dish

Kalbi is a dish in Korean cuisine mainly consisting of beef short ribs made with a marinated sauce usually containing soy sauce, garlic, and sugar.

Once the kalbi is cooked and ready to serve,It is usually sliced into pieces over the grill with kitchen scissors, optionally you can wrap the kalbi on lettuce leaves, kkaenip (perilla leaves), or other leafy vegetables, and is also normally accompanied with rice.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Asian (toasted) sesame oil
  • 2 pounds Korean-style short ribs (beef chuck flanken, cut 1/3 to 1/2 inch thick across bones)

Steps

  1. Stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, green onions, and sesame oil in a bowl until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Place ribs in a resealable plastic bag.Pour marinade over ribs. Seal the bag and refrigerate for 3 hours to overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat;lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove ribs from the bag;shake off excess marinade and discard marinade. Grill ribs on the preheated grill until meat is still pink but not bloody nearest the bone,5 to 7 minutes per side.
